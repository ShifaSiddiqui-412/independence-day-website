History and Significance

On 14th August 1947, Pakistan emerged as an independent nation, a day marked by the culmination of efforts led by Muhammad Ali Jinnah and other visionary leaders. This day symbolizes the sacrifices made for freedom and serves as a reminder of our commitment to uphold the values of our forefathers.

"With faith, discipline and selfless devotion to duty, there is nothing worthwhile that you cannot achieve." - Muhammad Ali Jinnah
